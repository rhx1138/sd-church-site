Our Beliefs

We are excited you are here; our goal is to "connect" you to God as you "connect" with us. We pray that you will find your purpose at Maranatha. With us, you can find lasting connections, help with growing your faith, and discover new opportunities for service

Communion

Join us for safe, pre-packaged Communion four times a year. 2nd Sabbath in January, 2nd Sabbath in April, 2nd Sabbath in July, and lastly 2nd Sabbath in October.

Mission

Our MISSION is to effectively share the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ to the San Diego communities and the surrounding areas.
(Rev. 14:6-12)

Vision

Our VISION is to be empowered witnesses for Christ.(Acts 1:8)
